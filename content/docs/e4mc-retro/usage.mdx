## How to use

Using e4mc Retro is seamless:

1.  **Install the mod** into your Minecraft instance.
2.  Start your world and click **Open to LAN**.
3.  A **public domain** will be generated and displayed in the chat alongside your local port.
4.  Share this domain with your friends, and they can connect directly to your LAN world.
